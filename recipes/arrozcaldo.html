Arroz Caldo

Description

Arroz Caldo is a popular Filipino rice porridge, a type of lugaw, that is often enjoyed for breakfast or as a comforting meal, especially when feeling under the weather. Arroz Caldo is known for its warm, comforting taste with a strong ginger flavor, balanced by the savory chicken broth and aromatics like garlic and onion.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil
  • 2-3 chicken breasts (cut into cubes is best)
  • 2 cups uncooked, washed rice
  • Peeled ginger root
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Water

Steps

  1. Saute chicken in garlic and oil in a large stock pot.
  2. Add rice into pot with chicken and fill pot halfway with water.
  3. Add ginger root and fish sauce.
  4. Bring it to a boil and boil until desired consistency (typically about 30 minutes to an hour)
